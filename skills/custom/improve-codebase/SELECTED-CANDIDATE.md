# Selected Improvement Candidate

Enter only for an explicit `Candidate N` and verified improvement report. [`SKILL.md`](SKILL.md) owns vocabulary and mutation boundaries.

**Reopen -> Verify -> Resolve -> Reclassify -> Route -> Reconcile -> Return.**

## Reopen

**Reopen.** Locate one stable candidate in the named report. Load its Source Trace, survey bound, disposition, proof seam, resolution need, and sequence relationship. Return an exact missing pointer for ambiguity. Do not repeat the Survey.

## Verify

**Verify.** Refresh repo and work state. Recheck load-bearing evidence against current source, callers, tests, history, domain context, and constraints; record material drift.

**Stale.** When the candidate leaves the surveyed bound, return a stale-candidate packet and recommend a new `$improve-codebase` survey.

## Resolve

**Resolve at most one blocker.** Follow its recorded need:

- **Repository:** inspect the missing source, caller, test, history, or configuration evidence.
- **Source:** invoke `$research` with one question, supported decision, scope, freshness, and `authorized note path: none` unless one path is approved. Receive cited evidence, one note pointer, or a blocker.
- **Runnable:** invoke `$prototype` with one terminal question, judgment authority, objective criteria when applicable, and a disposable `.tmp/improvement-prototypes/` path. App-tree or durable paths require explicit authority. Treat its reconciled verdict as design evidence, never production proof.
- **User decision:** invoke `$grill-with-docs` for one user-owned preference, commitment, material trade-off, domain term, context boundary, or ADR question. Receive its complete packet; `Evidence gap` stops resolution.
- **Design:** invoke `$codebase-design` only for a supported `Concentrate` candidate needing dependency, seam, ownership, interface, migration, or replacement design. Receive its design packet.

## Reclassify

**Reclassify.** Reapply the deletion test with returned evidence. Set `Eliminate`, `Concentrate`, `Retain`, or `Investigate`; a design verdict may change the original disposition. Unresolved conflict remains `Investigate`.

## Route

**Route one terminal.** Apply the stop-loss first, then the final disposition:

- **Multi-decision fog:** `$wayfinder` when multiple interdependent unresolved decisions or prerequisites need a tracker-backed route.
- **Eliminate:** `$simplify-code Candidate N from <absolute-report-path>`.
- **Concentrate, single-slice:** `$implement` when direction, commitment boundary, and proof are ready.
- **Concentrate, multi-slice:** `$to-tickets` when settled direction needs dependency-ordered slices.
- **Concentrate, specification-ready:** `$to-spec` when settled direction needs a durable parent specification before slicing.
- **Retain:** no change, with evidence that the boundary earns its cost.
- **Investigate:** exact gap, attempted resolver, result, and next evidence action; no downstream skill.

**Honor overlap.** Apply `Preparatory`, `Absorbed`, and `Residual` before routing so the recommendation duplicates no candidate work.

## Reconcile

**Reconcile.** Update the same card and Resolution section with resolver, returned evidence or decision, final disposition, route, and time. Preserve IDs, omit stale prototype paths, and verify under [HTML-REPORT.md](HTML-REPORT.md).

## Return

Return one packet containing:

- absolute report path, candidate ID, survey bound, and Source Trace;
- original and final dispositions with deletion-test result;
- resolver packet or direct evidence, resolution need, and limits;
- sequence relationship and overlap decision;
- recommended route and exact pickup, or no-change/evidence-gap verdict;
- report verification and downstream execution state.

## Completion

Complete when the candidate was reopened without resurveying; current evidence and any resolver packet were verified; reclassification and overlap closed; the report was reconciled; exactly one terminal route or verdict returned; and the parent Boundary held.
