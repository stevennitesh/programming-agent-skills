# Synthesis Preserves Exhaustive Research; Runtime Skills Compress

`docs/synthesis/` preserves exhaustive source pressure, current-runtime evidence, ownership decisions, alternatives, selected design, extraction placement, and validation plans. Compactness applies when that record is extracted into candidate wording and final runtime skills, not by pruning the synthesis record after behavior is chosen.

This separation keeps future skill rewrites explainable and prevents maintainers from rediscovering rejected options or silently changing ownership, while preserving the small semantic surface and low context load required of executable skills.

**Consequences**:
Whole-skill synthesis notes may use layered contracts, decision traces, rationale, and acceptance matrices. Runtime files remain executable authority and must carry only behavior-changing local contract slices; validation must prove the compressed wording rather than treating synthesis detail as shipped behavior.
