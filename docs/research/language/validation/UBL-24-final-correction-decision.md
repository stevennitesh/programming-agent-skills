  The correction does not label that shape a slicing failure.
- Full current-edition tracer-bullet text was not accessible beyond a licensed
  preview. The complete 2001 author article and official tips are sufficient

- No inspected source establishes frontier as general professional planning

- Live official documentation in EV-09 can change after 2026-07-22.
