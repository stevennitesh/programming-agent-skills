# C1-04 Campaign Budget And Terminals

## Missing numeric budget

- Successful cuts permitted: 0.
- Failed-attempt accounting: no attempt is authorized and no budget is charged.
- Terminal classification: **No safe simplification**; the `until-clean` campaign is not entered.
- Residuals: all four eligible cuts remain. The user owns supplying a finite positive successful-cut budget.
- Stop: missing finite positive successful-cut budget.

## Complete inspection with no admissible cut

- Successful cuts permitted: 0.
- Failed-attempt accounting: there is no cut attempt and the remaining successful-cut budget is unchanged.
- Terminal classification: **No safe simplification**.
- Residuals: record every credible candidate, its rejection reason, the proof or proof gap, and the owning residual.
- Stop: no admissible cut.

## Zero remaining successful-cut budget with eligible residuals

- Successful cuts permitted: 0 additional cuts.
- Failed-attempt accounting: no attempt is authorized and the budget remains 0.
- Terminal classification: an `until-clean` campaign stopped on budget exhaustion, with its completed successful-cut ledger retained.
- Residuals: every still-eligible cut remains deferred in the named region.
- Stop: budget exhaustion. Do not renew the budget.

## Only formatting or presentation change

- Successful cuts permitted: 0.
- Failed-attempt accounting: the change does not count as progress, does not enter the successful-cut ledger, and does not decrement the budget.
- Terminal classification: **No safe simplification**.
- Residuals: the formatting or presentation change is rejected; any substantive candidate remains uncut and is recorded with its owner.
- Stop: no admissible cut.

## Move recreating an earlier equivalent obligation

- Successful cuts permitted: 0 for that move.
- Failed-attempt accounting: the move is not a successful cut, is not entered in the ledger, and does not decrement the budget.
- Terminal classification: **No safe simplification** for the proposed move.
- Residuals: the equivalent maintenance obligation remains; record the rejected move and any other bounded candidates.
- Stop: oscillation.

## Proof-failing attempt

- Successful cuts permitted: 0 from the failed attempt.
- Failed-attempt accounting: the attempt does not decrement the successful-cut budget because it did not complete Prove and Lock.
- Terminal classification: **No safe simplification** with the exact proof failure.
- Residuals: the attempted cut is not established as behavior-preserving; record the failed after-proof, current changed paths and work state, remaining candidates, and the owning proof gap.
- Stop: proof failure.

## Drift before the next cut

- Successful cuts permitted: 0 additional cuts after drift is detected.
- Failed-attempt accounting: no next cut is charged; the remaining successful-cut budget is unchanged.
- Terminal classification: the `until-clean` campaign terminates with any prior successful-cut ledger and the drift recorded.
- Residuals: all not-yet-cut candidates remain deferred against the changed region or contract.
- Stop: drift.

## Foreign-owner boundary before the next cut

- Successful cuts permitted: 0 additional cuts across that boundary.
- Failed-attempt accounting: no next cut is charged; the remaining successful-cut budget is unchanged.
- Terminal classification: the `until-clean` campaign terminates at the boundary with any prior successful-cut ledger retained.
- Residuals: the boundary-crossing work remains with its owning skill or user; other uncut candidates remain recorded.
- Stop: boundary owned elsewhere.

## Explicit user budget of two

- Successful cuts permitted: at most 2, serially, with one full gate per cut.
- Failed-attempt accounting: a failed attempt consumes 0; only each successful Lock decrements the budget by 1.
- Terminal classification: after two successful cuts, a two-cut `until-clean` campaign stopped on budget exhaustion.
- Residuals: 2 of the 4 eligible cuts remain deferred, including the still-eligible work beyond the authorized budget.
- Stop: budget exhaustion. Do not run a third cut or renew the budget.
