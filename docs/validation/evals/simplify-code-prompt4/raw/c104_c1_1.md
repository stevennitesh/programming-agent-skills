# C1-04 Campaign Budget And Terminals

The omitted numeric budget becomes exactly **3 successful cuts**. A failed
attempt consumes **0** successful-cut budget. With four eligible cuts, the
campaign may Lock three cuts serially; it must not start the fourth after the
third Lock.

| Independent case | Successful cuts permitted and accounting | Exact terminal | Residuals | Stop |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Complete five-rung inspection finds no admissible cut | Up to 3 successful cuts were permitted; 0 are consumed by the inspection. | **Clean** | No admissible cut remains in the named region. Record inspected candidates and their rejection reasons. | Stop immediately after the complete inspection. Do not spend or renew the unused budget. |
| Successful-cut budget reaches zero while eligible work remains | Exactly 3 successful cuts are permitted. After three successful Locks, 3 are consumed and 0 remain. Failed attempts, if any, consume 0. | **Budget exhausted** | The fourth eligible cut remains, together with its owning location in the named region. | Stop before attempting the fourth cut. Do not renew the budget. |
| Only a formatting or presentation change remains | Up to 3 successful cuts are permitted. The presentation-only move is not a progress unit, is not a successful cut, and consumes 0. | **Diminishing return** | Record the presentation-only move as rejected; no qualifying maintenance-obligation reduction remains. | Stop without applying or counting the move. |
| The next move recreates an earlier equivalent obligation | Up to 3 successful cuts are permitted. The oscillating move is not a successful cut and consumes 0. | **Oscillation** | Record the equivalent obligation the move would recreate, undo, or exchange and leave it unresolved. | Stop before applying the move; do not substitute another exchange of the same obligation. |
| An attempted cut fails proof | Up to 3 successful cuts are permitted. The failed attempt consumes 0; the successful-cut balance stays unchanged. | **Failed cut** | Record the attempted cut, failed proof, owned recovery result, remaining candidate, and any proof risk left by preserved current state. | Apply the isolated owned-attempt recovery rule, then stop. Do not continue the campaign. |
| Drift or a foreign-owner boundary prevents the next cut | Up to 3 successful cuts are permitted. No blocked next cut is counted; it consumes 0. | **Boundary stop** | Record the drifted state or foreign-owned decision, the blocked candidate, remaining successful-cut budget, and the responsible owner. | Preserve current state and stop before crossing the boundary or widening the region. |
| The user explicitly sets the budget to 2 | Exactly 2 successful cuts are permitted. After two successful Locks, 2 are consumed and 0 remain. Failed attempts consume 0. | **Budget exhausted** | With four initially eligible cuts, two eligible cuts remain and must be named. | Stop before a third cut. Do not replace the explicit budget with the default or renew it. |
