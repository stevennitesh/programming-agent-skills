# C1-04 Campaign Budget And Terminals

Failed attempts never consume successful-cut budget. Budget decrements only
after a cut completes `Lock` with a strict monotonic reduction.

| Case | Successful cuts permitted | Failed-attempt accounting | Terminal classification | Residuals and stop |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| `until-clean` with no numeric budget | 0 | No attempt is admitted, so 0 is charged. | Campaign not entered; no selected simplification outcome. | All four eligible cuts remain. Stop because the required finite positive successful-cut budget is missing; request that the user supply it. |
| Complete inspection finds no admissible cut | 0 | Any rejected candidate charges 0. | **No safe simplification**. | Report the inspected region, rejected candidates and reasons, proof state or gap, work state, and owning residual. Stop on no admissible cut. |
| Zero budget remains while eligible cuts remain | 0 additional cuts | Any failed attempts have charged 0; prior successful Locks alone consumed the budget. | Until-clean campaign stopped by budget exhaustion. | Report every eligible residual without executing it. Stop immediately and do not renew the budget. |
| Only a formatting or presentation change is available | 0 | The change cannot be recorded as a successful cut and charges 0. | **No safe simplification**. | Record the formatting/presentation-only candidate as rejected; it is not campaign progress. Stop on no admissible cut. |
| A move recreates an earlier equivalent obligation | 0 for that move | The move does not complete a successful monotonic Lock and charges 0. | Until-clean campaign stopped by oscillation. | Preserve or restore the last proved state, report the recreated obligation and all still-eligible residuals, and stop without retrying the equivalent move. |
| An attempted cut fails proof | 0 for that attempt | The failed attempt charges 0; the remaining successful-cut budget is unchanged. | Until-clean campaign stopped by proof failure. | Return to the last proved state, identify the failed proof and the unproved candidate, report remaining residuals and risk, and stop. |
| Drift occurs before the next cut | 0 after drift | No successful Lock occurred, so 0 is charged. | Until-clean campaign stopped by drift. | Report the changed evidence or boundary and all unprocessed residuals. Stop before mutation or retracing under a widened scope. |
| A foreign-owner boundary is reached before the next cut | 0 beyond the boundary | No successful Lock occurred, so 0 is charged. | Until-clean campaign stopped at a boundary owned elsewhere. | Name the owning residual and report all other unprocessed residuals. Stop without copying the foreign owner's procedure. |
| Explicit user budget of two, with four eligible cuts | At most 2 | Failed attempts charge 0; each successful Lock charges 1. | After two successful Locks, until-clean campaign stopped by budget exhaustion. | Two eligible cuts remain and are reported as residuals. Stop after the second Lock; do not renew the budget. |
