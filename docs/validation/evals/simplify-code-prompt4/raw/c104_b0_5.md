# C1-04 B0 Control Sample 5

| Case | Successful cuts permitted | Failed-attempt budget accounting | Terminal classification | Residuals and stop |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| `until-clean` names a region but supplies no numeric budget | 0 | No budget exists to debit. | **No safe simplification** — missing finite positive successful-cut budget. | All four eligible cuts remain residual. Do not enter the campaign; stop before the first cut and return budget selection to the user. |
| Complete inspection finds no admissible cut | 0 further cuts | No unsuccessful candidate or attempt decrements the budget. | **No safe simplification** — no admissible cut. | Record the inspected region, credible candidates, rejection reasons, proof state, work state, and owning residual; stop on no admissible cut. |
| Zero successful-cut budget remains while eligible cuts remain | 0 further cuts | Failed attempts consume 0; the remaining budget stays 0. | Campaign terminal — **budget exhaustion**. | Report every eligible cut as residual, preserve the successful-cut ledger, and stop without renewing the budget. Further commitment belongs to the user. |
| Only a formatting or presentation change is available | 0 | The change is not progress and consumes 0 successful-cut budget. | **No safe simplification** — no admissible cut. | The maintenance obligations remain residual; stop because formatting and presentation-only changes cannot count as campaign progress. |
| A move recreates an earlier equivalent obligation | 0 for that move | The move is not a successful Lock and consumes 0 successful-cut budget. | Campaign terminal — **oscillation**. | Preserve the last proved monotonic state, record the equivalent obligation as residual, and stop without retrying the cycle. |
| An attempted cut fails proof | 0 for that attempt | A failed proof does not decrement the successful-cut budget. Only a successful Lock decrements it. | Campaign terminal — **proof failure**. | Report the before proof, failed after proof, affected paths, work state, skipped proof or risk, and remaining budget; stop at the failed attempt. |
| Drift occurs before the next cut | 0 further cuts | No next successful Lock occurred, so the budget is unchanged. | Campaign terminal — **drift**. | Record the drift, current ledger, remaining budget, and eligible residuals; stop before another cut. |
| A foreign-owner boundary is reached before the next cut | 0 further cuts | No next successful Lock occurred, so the budget is unchanged. | Campaign terminal — **boundary owned elsewhere**. | Return the boundary and residual to its owner with the current ledger and remaining budget; stop before copying or performing the foreign procedure. |
| The user explicitly supplies a budget of 2 | At most 2 successful cuts | Each successful Lock decrements the budget by 1. Every failed attempt consumes 0. | Campaign terminal after two successful Locks — **budget exhaustion**. | Two of the four eligible cuts remain residual. Report both successful cuts in the serial ledger and stop without renewing the budget. |

For any case following prior successful cuts, retain those cuts in the ledger and retain their consumed budget. A terminal attempt adds no successful cut, and the exact terminal reason above ends the serial campaign.
