# C1-04 Campaign Budget And Terminals

## Omitted budget with four eligible cuts

- Successful cuts permitted: 3, the default finite budget.
- Failed-attempt accounting: each failed attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget.
- Campaign course: lock 3 successful cuts serially; do not take the fourth.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: 1 eligible cut remains in the named region.
- Stop: stop immediately after the third successful Lock because no successful-cut budget remains.

## Complete inspection with no admissible cut

- Successful cuts permitted: up to 3 by the default budget; 0 are taken.
- Failed-attempt accounting: 0 budget is consumed by any failed attempt.
- Terminal: **Clean**.
- Residuals: no admissible cut remains after the complete five-rung inspection.
- Stop: stop when that inspection completes; do not spend or renew the unused budget.

## Zero remaining budget with eligible residuals

- Successful cuts permitted: 0 more.
- Failed-attempt accounting: a failed attempt would consume 0 successful-cut budget, but it cannot authorize another attempt after exhaustion.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: report every still-eligible cut in the named region.
- Stop: stop before another cut because no successful-cut budget remains.

## Formatting or presentation only

- Successful cuts permitted: up to 3 by the default budget; 0 are taken for the presentation-only move.
- Failed-attempt accounting: 0 budget is consumed by a failed attempt; the presentation-only move is not a successful cut.
- Terminal: **Diminishing return**.
- Residuals: the strongest remaining move changes presentation only and supplies no progress unit.
- Stop: do not apply or count the move; stop without decrementing the budget.

## Move recreates an earlier equivalent obligation

- Successful cuts permitted: up to 3 by the default budget; the recreating move is not permitted as a successful cut.
- Failed-attempt accounting: 0 successful-cut budget is consumed by a rejected or failed attempt.
- Terminal: **Oscillation**.
- Residuals: the existing obligation remains; the proposed move would exchange it for an equivalent earlier obligation.
- Stop: do not apply or lock the move, and do not decrement or renew the budget.

## Proof-failing attempt

- Successful cuts permitted: up to 3 by the default budget; the proof-failing attempt contributes 0 successful cuts.
- Failed-attempt accounting: the failed attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget, leaving the successful-cut budget unchanged.
- Terminal: **Failed cut**.
- Residuals: the attempted cut remains unclaimed, and the unresolved proof failure remains the proof gap.
- Stop: follow attempt-owned recovery for the isolated attempt, preserve current state when recovery cannot be isolated, and stop without locking the attempt.

## Drift or foreign-owner boundary before the next cut

- Successful cuts permitted: up to the campaign's remaining successful-cut budget; 0 additional cuts are taken across the boundary.
- Failed-attempt accounting: 0 successful-cut budget is consumed because no successful Lock occurs.
- Terminal: **Boundary stop**.
- Residuals: report the deferred candidate, the blocking drift or proof/region limit, and the foreign owner when ownership is the boundary.
- Stop: stop before the next cut; do not widen the region, cross ownership, or renew the budget.

## Explicit user budget of two

- Successful cuts permitted: exactly 2.
- Failed-attempt accounting: each failed attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget.
- Campaign course: lock 2 successful cuts serially; do not take a third.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: 2 of the 4 eligible cuts remain in the named region.
- Stop: stop immediately after the second successful Lock because no successful-cut budget remains.
