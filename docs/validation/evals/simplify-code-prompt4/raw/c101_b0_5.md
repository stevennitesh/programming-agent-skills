# C1-01 B0 Control Sample 5

Mode: one bounded Simplify Code decision, read-only.

## Positive

- **Selected cut:** Delete the expired branch proven unreachable.
- **Stop:** This is the first admissible rung. Do not consider Reuse,
  Standardize, Collapse, or Shrink.
- **Proof boundary:** The authoritative reachability evidence must cover every
  operational entry and caller of the branch. The caller-facing fixture is the
  observable seam: it must pass against the exact starting state and again
  after the prospective deletion, followed by the nearest relevant checks.
- **State boundary:** Decision only. No cut is made; the working tree, index,
  starting ref, unrelated state, installed state, and external state remain
  unchanged.

## Companion 1

- **Selected cut:** Reuse the exact project-owned semantic owner.
- **Stop:** Delete is stopped by the authoritative semantic condition. Reuse is
  the next admissible rung; do not descend further.
- **Proof boundary:** The owner must be semantically exact at the caller-facing
  seam, not merely textually similar. The same fixture must establish the
  baseline and detect any caller-visible change after the prospective
  replacement.
- **State boundary:** Decision only. No repository or external state is
  changed, and the index and unrelated work remain as found.
