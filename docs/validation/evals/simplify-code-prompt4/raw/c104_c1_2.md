# C1-04 Campaign Budget And Terminals

## Omitted budget; four eligible cuts

- Successful cuts permitted: 3, the default finite budget.
- Failed-attempt accounting: each failed attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget.
- Ledger at stop: 3 successful cuts; 0 budget remaining.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: the fourth eligible cut remains eligible and unperformed.
- Stop: stop immediately after the third successful Lock. Do not inspect as Clean, attempt the fourth cut, or renew the budget.

## Complete inspection with no admissible cut

- Successful cuts permitted by budget: up to 3; successful cuts performed in this case: 0.
- Failed-attempt accounting: any failed attempt would consume 0 successful-cut budget.
- Terminal: **Clean**.
- Residuals: no admissible cut remains in the named region after the complete five-rung inspection.
- Stop: stop at the completed inspection; unused budget does not require another attempt.

## Zero remaining budget with eligible residuals

- Successful cuts permitted from the current state: 0.
- Failed-attempt accounting: failed attempts consume 0 budget, but no further attempt starts after exhaustion.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: report every still-eligible cut as unperformed; eligibility does not authorize spending beyond the budget.
- Stop: stop before the next cut.

## Formatting or presentation change only

- Successful cuts permitted by budget: up to 3; this change counts as 0 successful cuts.
- Failed-attempt accounting: 0 budget is consumed.
- Terminal: **Diminishing return**.
- Residuals: the presentation-only move remains unperformed and is not a progress unit; report any other known eligible work separately.
- Stop: stop without making or locking the presentation-only change.

## Move recreates an earlier equivalent obligation

- Successful cuts permitted by budget: up to 3; the recreating move counts as 0 successful cuts.
- Failed-attempt accounting: 0 budget is consumed.
- Terminal: **Oscillation**.
- Residuals: the move remains unperformed because it recreates, undoes, or exchanges an earlier obligation for an equivalent one.
- Stop: stop before that move; do not continue the campaign by cycling obligations.

## Proof-failing attempt

- Successful cuts permitted by budget: up to 3; the proof-failing attempt counts as 0 successful cuts.
- Failed-attempt accounting: the attempt consumes no successful-cut budget, so the pre-attempt remaining budget is unchanged.
- Terminal: **Failed cut**.
- Residuals: the attempted candidate is not locked; report it and every unattempted eligible cut as residual work, together with the proof failure and owned recovery state.
- Stop: stop after applying the isolated owned-attempt recovery rule; an unchanged budget does not authorize continuing past this terminal.

## Drift or foreign-owner boundary before the next cut

- Successful cuts permitted by budget: up to the remaining amount; the blocked next cut consumes 0.
- Failed-attempt accounting: no successful-cut budget is consumed.
- Terminal: **Boundary stop**.
- Residuals: report the blocked cut and later eligible cuts, assigning the drift resolution or foreign-owned work to its owner.
- Stop: stop before mutation; do not widen the region or cross ownership to continue.

## Explicit user budget of two

- Successful cuts permitted: 2.
- Failed-attempt accounting: each failed attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget.
- Ledger at stop: 2 successful cuts; 0 budget remaining.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: with four eligible cuts, the third and fourth remain eligible and unperformed.
- Stop: stop immediately after the second successful Lock; do not apply the omitted-budget default or renew the explicit budget.
