# C1-04 B0 Decisions

## Explicit `until-clean`, named region, no numeric budget

- Successful cuts permitted: **0**. A finite positive successful-cut budget is
  required before entering `until-clean`; the four eligible cuts do not supply
  or imply that budget.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: no attempt is authorized, so no failed
  attempt is charged. A failed attempt would not be a successful Lock and
  therefore would not decrement a successful-cut budget.
- Terminal classification: **No safe simplification — campaign not admitted:
  missing finite positive successful-cut budget**.
- Residuals: all four eligible cuts remain. The user owns the missing budget
  commitment.
- Stop: before the campaign gate and before any production mutation. Do not
  infer, renew, or choose a budget.

## Complete inspection with no admissible cut

- Successful cuts permitted: **0 at this state**.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: no failed attempt is charged; the remaining
  successful-cut budget is unchanged.
- Terminal classification: **no admissible cut**.
- Residuals: record the credible candidates, their rejection reasons, and the
  owning residual; there is no admissible cut to carry forward.
- Stop: after `Choose`, without mutation. Return **No safe simplification** if
  the campaign completed no successful cut.

## Zero remaining successful-cut budget with eligible residuals

- Successful cuts permitted: **0 further cuts**.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: failed attempts do not consume
  successful-cut budget and cannot create additional budget.
- Terminal classification: **budget exhaustion**.
- Residuals: record every still-eligible cut, including the remaining fourth
  cut, as deferred work. A future campaign requires a new user-owned budget
  commitment.
- Stop: immediately when the ledger reaches zero, before the next cut. Do not
  renew the budget.

## Only a formatting or presentation change

- Successful cuts permitted: **0 for that change**.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: it consumes no successful-cut budget
  because formatting or presentation-only work is not campaign progress.
- Terminal classification: **no admissible cut** when it is the only remaining
  candidate.
- Residuals: record the formatting or presentation change as rejected or
  deferred; the maintenance-obligation ledger remains unchanged.
- Stop: before mutation, or remove any invocation-created presentation-only
  change before returning. Do not decrement the budget.

## Move that recreates an earlier equivalent obligation

- Successful cuts permitted: **0 for the move**.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: the move does not receive a successful
  Lock and does not decrement the successful-cut budget.
- Terminal classification: **oscillation**.
- Residuals: the equivalent obligation remains at the last successfully locked
  state; record the move and the recreated obligation.
- Stop: when equivalence to the earlier obligation is established. Do not
  accept the move as a strict monotonic reduction.

## Proof-failing attempt

- Successful cuts permitted: **0 for the failed attempt**.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: **no decrement**. Only a cut that completes
  `Prove -> Lock` consumes one unit of successful-cut budget.
- Terminal classification: **proof failure**.
- Residuals: the candidate remains unresolved at the last successfully locked
  state; report the exact failed proof, skipped proof or risk, and affected
  residual owner.
- Stop: immediately on proof failure. Remove the attempted cut and its
  invocation-created fallout, preserve the index and unrelated work, and do
  not proceed to another cut.

## Drift before the next cut

- Successful cuts permitted: **0 further cuts after drift is detected**.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: no decrement for the uncompleted next cut;
  already locked successful cuts remain charged.
- Terminal classification: **drift**.
- Residuals: record the remaining candidates against the last successfully
  locked state and identify the changed condition that invalidated continuation.
- Stop: before the next cut. Do not widen or redefine the admitted region.

## Foreign-owner boundary before the next cut

- Successful cuts permitted: **0 further cuts across that boundary**.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: no decrement for the uncompleted next cut;
  already locked successful cuts remain charged.
- Terminal classification: **boundary owned elsewhere**.
- Residuals: record the remaining candidate and its foreign owner.
- Stop: before copying the foreign owner's procedure or mutating across the
  boundary; return the residual to that owner.

## Explicit user budget of two

- Successful cuts permitted: **at most 2**, serially, with one complete
  `Trace -> Baseline -> Choose -> Cut -> Prove -> Lock` gate per cut.
- Failed-attempt budget accounting: a failed attempt consumes **0** units. Each
  successful Lock consumes exactly one unit.
- Terminal classification after two successful Locks: **budget exhaustion**.
- Residuals: with four eligible cuts, two eligible cuts remain after the second
  successful Lock. Record both as deferred; the user owns any new campaign
  budget.
- Stop: immediately after the second successful Lock and ledger entry. Do not
  start the third cut or renew the budget.
