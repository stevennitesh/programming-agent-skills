# C1-04 Campaign Budget And Terminals

The successful-cut budget counts only cuts that complete `Trace -> Baseline -> Choose -> Cut -> Prove -> Lock`. A failed attempt consumes no successful-cut budget.

| Case | Successful cuts permitted | Budget accounting | Terminal | Residuals and stop |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Budget omitted; four eligible cuts | 3 | The default budget is 3. Each successful Lock consumes one unit; after the third Lock, 0 remains. | **Budget exhausted** | The fourth eligible cut is reported as an eligible residual. Stop before attempting it; do not renew the budget. |
| Complete five-rung inspection finds no admissible cut | 0 further cuts | No budget unit is consumed by inspection. Any unspent budget remains unspent. | **Clean** | There is no admissible residual cut in the named region. Stop after the complete inspection. |
| Zero remaining budget with eligible residuals | 0 further cuts | The successful-cut budget is already exhausted. Inspection and reporting consume no unit. | **Budget exhausted** | Report every known eligible residual cut. Stop before another attempt; do not renew the budget. |
| Only a formatting or presentation change remains | 0 further cuts | Presentation-only work is not a progress unit and consumes no successful-cut budget. | **Diminishing return** | Report the presentation-only change as a non-progress residual, not as an eligible simplification. Stop without making or counting it. |
| A move recreates an earlier equivalent obligation | 0 further cuts for that move | The move cannot enter the successful-cut ledger and consumes no successful-cut budget. | **Oscillation** | Report the rejected move and the equivalent obligation it would recreate, undo, or exchange. Stop without making or locking the move. |
| A proof-failing attempt | 0 successful cuts from the attempt | The failed attempt consumes no successful-cut budget. Reverse only the isolated owned attempt when current bytes still match its recorded result; otherwise preserve current state. | **Failed cut** | Report the attempted cut, proof failure, last locked state, remaining budget, and unattempted eligible residuals. Stop after the owned recovery rule; do not continue the campaign. |
| Drift or a foreign-owner boundary prevents the next cut | 0 further cuts | No unit is consumed for a cut that cannot begin or Lock. The remaining successful-cut budget stays unchanged. | **Boundary stop** | Report the blocked candidate, remaining eligible residuals, and the drifted or foreign-owned boundary and owner. Stop at that boundary; do not widen the region. |
| Explicit user budget of 2; four eligible cuts | 2 | Each successful Lock consumes one unit; after the second Lock, 0 remains. | **Budget exhausted** | Report the two still-eligible cuts as residuals. Stop before the third cut; do not renew the budget. |
