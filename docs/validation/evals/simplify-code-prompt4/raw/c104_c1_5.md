# C1-04 Campaign Budget And Terminals

## Omitted budget with four eligible cuts

- Successful cuts permitted: exactly 3. The omitted numeric budget defaults to 3 successful cuts.
- Failed-attempt accounting: a failed attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget.
- Sequence: run and Lock the cuts serially. After the third successful Lock, remaining budget is 0.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: the fourth eligible cut remains eligible and must be reported.
- Stop: stop immediately after the third successful Lock. Do not attempt the fourth cut, widen the region, or renew the budget.

## Complete inspection finds no admissible cut

- Successful cuts permitted: at most the 3 held by the default budget, but 0 further cuts are authorized by this inspection.
- Failed-attempt accounting: any failed attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget.
- Terminal: **Clean**, provided the inspection covered all five rungs.
- Residuals: no admissible cut. Record the inspected candidates and their rejection reasons; do not report a rejected candidate as eligible.
- Stop: stop when the complete five-rung inspection establishes that no admissible cut exists, even if successful-cut budget remains.

## Zero remaining budget with eligible residuals

- Successful cuts permitted: 0 additional successful cuts.
- Failed-attempt accounting: failed attempts consumed 0; only successful Locks exhausted the budget.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: report every still-eligible cut without attempting it.
- Stop: stop before another Cut.

## Only a formatting or presentation change remains

- Successful cuts permitted: the default campaign cap remains 3, but this move is not a permitted successful cut and decrements no budget.
- Failed-attempt accounting: 0 budget consumed.
- Terminal: **Diminishing return**.
- Residuals: record the formatting or presentation-only move as non-progress, not as an eligible simplification.
- Stop: do not make or count the presentation-only change.

## Proposed move recreates an earlier equivalent obligation

- Successful cuts permitted: the default campaign cap remains 3, but this move cannot be Locked as a successful cut.
- Failed-attempt accounting: the rejected move consumes 0 successful-cut budget.
- Terminal: **Oscillation**.
- Residuals: identify the earlier obligation and the equivalent obligation the move would recreate, undo, or exchange.
- Stop: stop before making the oscillating move; do not substitute an equivalent obligation or renew the budget.

## Proof-failing attempt

- Successful cuts permitted: the default campaign cap is 3; the proof-failing attempt produces 0 successful Locks.
- Failed-attempt accounting: the attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget, so the remaining budget is unchanged.
- Terminal: **Failed cut** when the attempt cannot restore proof and the owned recovery rule is satisfied.
- Residuals: report the attempted cut, the failed proof, the recovery result, the unchanged successful-cut budget, and any state that must remain with its owner.
- Stop: refresh current bytes, apply only the owned isolated recovery allowed by the rule, preserve current state when isolation or byte identity is not established, and stop without another cut.

## Drift or foreign-owner boundary before the next cut

- Successful cuts permitted: no additional cut is permitted after the boundary is encountered; any unused portion of the default cap remains unused.
- Failed-attempt accounting: 0 successful-cut budget is consumed by detecting drift or foreign ownership.
- Terminal: **Boundary stop**.
- Residuals: for drift, report the affected current state and the evidence needed from its owner. For foreign ownership, report the eligible or proposed residual and name its owner.
- Stop: stop before mutation. Do not absorb the drift, cross the region limit, copy the foreign owner's procedure, widen the region, or renew the budget.

## Explicit user budget of two

- Successful cuts permitted: exactly 2.
- Failed-attempt accounting: each failed attempt consumes 0 successful-cut budget.
- Sequence: run serially and decrement only after each successful Lock. With four eligible cuts, the second successful Lock leaves 0 budget.
- Terminal: **Budget exhausted**.
- Residuals: the two unattempted eligible cuts remain and must be reported.
- Stop: stop immediately after the second successful Lock. Do not attempt a third cut or replace the explicit budget with the default.
