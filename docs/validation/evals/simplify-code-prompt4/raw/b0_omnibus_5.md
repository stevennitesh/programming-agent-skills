5. **Finite `until-clean` campaigns.** Admit only `src/parser/` and hold one invariant behavior contract and caller-facing proof seam. For every candidate, run serially and irreversibly: `Trace -> Baseline -> Choose -> Cut -> Prove -> Lock`; never parallelize or widen. After each successful Lock, record removed and introduced maintenance obligations, require strict monotonic net reduction, preserve an unstaged result and all unrelated/index state, and decrement the successful-cut budget. With budget **3**, perform at most three eligible proved cuts, each against the prior locked result, and stop on budget exhaustion after the third; the fourth remains residual. Either campaign stops earlier on no admissible cut, proof failure, drift, oscillation, or an outside-owner boundary, without renewing the budget. Return the region, initial budget, per-cut ledger, before/after proof for every cut, changed paths, starting/ending state, rejected formatting candidate, deferred cuts, exact terminal reason, skipped proof/risk, and named residual owner; further budget authorization belongs to the user. Completion additionally requires the serial ledger and terminal reason to match the evidence. Invocation classification: explicit `until-clean`, with both named finite positive budgets valid.
