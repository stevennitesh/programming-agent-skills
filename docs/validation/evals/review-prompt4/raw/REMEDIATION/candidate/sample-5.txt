# Remediation Scope

Review is limited to carried outcomes `F1`–`F4`, the caller-owned Repair delta and its affected seams, and the remaining acceptance exercised at those seams. Unrelated untouched outcome `U` is outside scope and is not reopened.

# Carried Dispositions

- `F1`: `resolved`
- `F2`: `still admitted`
- `F3`: `disproved`
- `F4`: `incomplete`
