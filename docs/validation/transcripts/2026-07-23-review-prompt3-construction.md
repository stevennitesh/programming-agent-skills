Review judges one bounded ordinary branch, WIP, staged, or since-X diff from
one complete immutable snapshot. It routes local PRs and high-risk local diffs
to Convergent PR Review and leaves repository-baseline audits to Audit
Codebase. It traces Standards and applicable Spec, judges them as separate
axes with a lens reset, verifies candidate observations through the shared
finding gates, detects drift, and returns one complete or incomplete report.

Review is read-only. The caller retains the Charter, target inputs, proof and
risk decisions, Repair, Lock, successor snapshots, delivery, trackers, and
every mutation. `incomplete` is the safe failure when routing, capture,
required sources or evidence, drift, or truthful reporting cannot close.
